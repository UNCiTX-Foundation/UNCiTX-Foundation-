Our Work

Empowering communities through innovation, education, neo-sustainability, and social impact initiatives.

What We Do

UNCITX Foundation designs and implements projects that address critical social challenges in India and beyond. Our focus areas include education, technology, environment, rural development, and community empowerment.

Impact & Achievements

Education Programs

Supported over 100 students in rural areas with digital learning resources.

Community Health

Organized health camps, vaccination drives, and awareness campaigns in underserved regions.

Environmental Initiatives

Planted 1000+ trees and conducted river clean-up drives across Assam.

Technology & Innovation

Developed local apps and digital tools for education and civic engagement.
