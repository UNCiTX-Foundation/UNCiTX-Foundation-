Who We Are

UNCITX Foundation was established in 2025 by Shivanta Ronghang with the vision of creating a universal nexus of science, innovation, and humanity. Our mission is to empower communities, promote rational thinking, neo-mobility, neo-resilience and drive neo-sustainable development through evidence-based approaches and collaborative initiatives.

We believe in the power of collective intelligence and interdisciplinary collaboration to solve complex global challenges. Our team comprises scientists, educators, community leaders, and volunteers who share a common goal of creating a more equitable and neo-sustainable future.

Our Philosophy

Based on the Civilization 3.0 concept, our philosophy combines scientific thinking, humanitarian principles, and evidence-based action to bring positive change to society. We envision a world where technology serves humanity, education 5.0 is accessible to all, and communities thrive in harmony with their environment.

Our approach is grounded in three core principles: rational inquiry, compassionate action, and sustainable innovation. We believe that lasting change requires addressing root causes rather than symptoms, and that solutions must be co-created with the communities they are meant to serve.
